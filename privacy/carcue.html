Privacy Policy — CarCue

Effective date: 25 June 2026 · Developer: N5 Labs · Contact: support@n5labs.com
Short version: CarCue is a local-first car utility for parked-car location, exit reminders, widgets, and startup-sound setup. Your parking notes, reminder labels, vehicle badges, family profile names, and audio files stay on your device. We do not request Contacts, we do not sell or share personal data, and we do not track you across apps or websites.

1. Who we are

N5 Labs ("we", "us") is an independent iOS developer. CarCue helps drivers save where they parked, run an exit checklist, arm back-seat reminders, organize car widgets, and set up startup sounds through iOS Shortcuts.

2. What stays on your device

  • Parked-car details, including level, spot, notes, and recent parking history, are stored locally on your device.
  • Reminder labels, checklist state, child reminder status, service reminders, parking meter state, vehicle badges, and family profiles are stored locally.
  • Imported startup-sound files and saved sound profiles are stored locally. We do not upload audio files or filenames.
  • Widget state is written to Apple's App Group container so the CarCue app and CarCue widgets can read the same local state.

3. Location

CarCue asks for "When In Use" location only when you save where your car is parked or open parking directions. Location is used to save a parking point, show it on the map, and open Apple Maps walking directions. CarCue does not use background location, does not continuously track driving, and does not send precise parking coordinates to our analytics.

4. Notifications

If you allow notifications, CarCue schedules local notifications for Exit Guard, child reminder, parking meter, and service reminders. These reminders are created on your device through Apple's notification system.

5. Analytics

CarCue may send privacy-safe product interaction analytics to help us understand whether core setup steps work. If analytics is enabled in a release build, CarCue uses a random app-scoped analytics identifier so events can be counted without an account. Analytics events never include coordinates, parking notes, child labels, imported audio filenames, profile names, driver names, vehicle nicknames, brand, model, or year. Analytics are not used for tracking across apps or websites.

6. Purchases

CarCue Pro is sold through Apple's App Store using StoreKit. Apple processes payment information. We do not see your credit card, Apple ID password, or billing details. The app stores only whether Pro is unlocked.

7. Permissions CarCue does not request

  • No Contacts access.
  • No camera or microphone access.
  • No Photos access for widgets or dashboard customization.
  • No advertising identifier and no App Tracking Transparency tracking prompt.

8. Children

CarCue includes a caregiver-controlled back-seat reminder. It does not detect people in the car, does not use a camera or passenger sensor, and is not a child monitoring service. CarCue is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Your control

You can delete local CarCue data by clearing saved items in the app or deleting the app. You can change location and notification permissions in iOS Settings. For privacy questions, email support@n5labs.com.

10. Changes

We may update this policy as the app evolves. Material changes will be reflected here with a new effective date.
