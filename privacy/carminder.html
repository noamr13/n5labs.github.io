Privacy Policy — CarMinder

Effective date: 26 June 2026 · Developer: N5 Labs · Contact: support@n5labs.com
Short version: CarMinder is a local-first car dashboard and reminder app. It does not request location permission in V1 and does not track your parked-car location. Apple Maps handles parked-car location when you enable that Apple Maps feature.

1. Who we are

N5 Labs ("we", "us") is an independent iOS developer. CarMinder is an iPhone app for car dashboard customization, Backseat Check reminders, and Apple Maps parked-car setup guidance.

2. Data stored on your device

CarMinder may store these settings locally on your device:

  • Car nickname, model name, dashboard color, and dashboard style.
  • Selected reminder duration and reminder sound.
  • Active Backseat Check reminder state.
  • Whether you marked Apple Maps parked-car setup as complete.
  • Pro entitlement state used to unlock paid features.
  • An anonymous analytics install identifier when analytics is configured.

3. Notifications

CarMinder uses local notifications for Backseat Check reminders. Notification permission is requested only when you start a reminder. You can change notification permissions in iOS Settings.

4. Location

CarMinder does not request location permission in V1 and does not track your car location.

Parked-car location is handled by Apple Maps. CarMinder only provides setup guidance and a handoff back to Apple Maps.

5. Purchases

CarMinder may offer optional Pro features through auto-renewable subscriptions sold by Apple through your App Store account. We use RevenueCat to manage subscription entitlements and validate purchase status. RevenueCat may process purchase identifiers and subscription history needed to unlock Pro features. We never see your payment card or Apple ID password.

See RevenueCat's privacy policy and Apple's privacy policy.

6. Analytics

CarMinder can send product analytics events through PostHog when an analytics key is configured. Events are designed to avoid personal content, exact location, license plates, phone numbers, names, prompts, messages, and audio URLs. We do not use the advertising identifier (IDFA).

7. Children

CarMinder is a general utility and reminder app. It is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. No sale of personal information

N5 Labs does not sell personal information.

9. Your choices

You can remove locally stored CarMinder data by deleting the app from your device. For questions or requests, email support@n5labs.com.

10. Changes

We may update this policy as the app evolves. Material changes will be reflected here with a new effective date.
