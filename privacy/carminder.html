Privacy Policy — CarMinder

Effective date: 26 June 2026 · Developer: N5 Labs · Contact: support@n5labs.com
Short version: CarMinder is a local-first car dashboard widget app. It stores your widget setup on your device, shares widget data with its WidgetKit extension, and uses Apple Shortcuts for optional ride sound automations.

1. Who we are

N5 Labs ("we", "us") is an independent iOS developer. CarMinder is an iPhone app for car dashboard widgets, lyrics-style cards, motion-poster cards, and ride sound setup.

2. Data stored on your device

CarMinder may store these settings locally on your device:

  • Car nickname, model name, city, dashboard color, and selected widget style.
  • Selected car image and widget preview data used by the WidgetKit extension.
  • Lyrics-card text, song title, artist label, theme, and line progress.
  • Motion-poster title, subtitle, frame style, and selected frame.
  • Selected ride sounds and imported custom sound files for CarPlay connected, CarPlay disconnected, and home arrival cues.
  • Pro entitlement state used to unlock paid features.
  • An anonymous analytics install identifier when analytics is configured.

3. Widgets and photos

When you choose a car image, CarMinder processes it on device into a bounded widget image and stores it in the app's shared container so the WidgetKit extension can display it. CarMinder does not request broad photo-library permission in V1.

4. Notifications and Shortcuts

CarMinder can provide local notification sounds for Apple Shortcuts automations. Notification permission may be requested when you enable or run sound cues. Imported custom sounds stay on your device and are used only for local sound cues. You can change notification permissions in iOS Settings.

Apple Shortcuts runs personal automations such as CarPlay connect, CarPlay disconnect, and Arrive. CarMinder provides App Shortcuts plus bundled or user-imported local sound cues; it does not control the CarPlay interface.

5. Location

CarMinder does not request location permission in V1 and does not track your location. Home arrival automations are configured and run by Apple's Shortcuts app.

6. Purchases

CarMinder may offer optional Pro features through auto-renewable subscriptions sold by Apple through your App Store account. We use RevenueCat to manage subscription entitlements and validate purchase status. RevenueCat may process purchase identifiers and subscription history needed to unlock Pro features. We never see your payment card or Apple ID password.

See RevenueCat's privacy policy and Apple's privacy policy.

7. Analytics

CarMinder can send product analytics events through PostHog when an analytics key is configured. Events are designed to avoid personal content, exact location, license plates, phone numbers, names, prompts, messages, and audio URLs. We do not use the advertising identifier (IDFA).

8. Children

CarMinder is a general lifestyle utility. It is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. No sale of personal information

N5 Labs does not sell personal information.

10. Your choices

You can remove locally stored CarMinder data by deleting the app from your device. For questions or requests, email support@n5labs.com.

11. Changes

We may update this policy as the app evolves. Material changes will be reflected here with a new effective date.
