Privacy Policy — Critter Camp

Effective date: 13 May 2026 · Developer: N5 Labs · Contact: support@n5labs.com
Short version: Critter Camp is a focus-friendly games app for children ages 6–12. We do not collect personal information from your child. We do not show ads. We do not sell or share data. We use a small amount of anonymous analytics to know whether the app is working, and we use Apple's App Store + RevenueCat to manage optional parent-purchased subscriptions.

1. Who we are

N5 Labs ("we", "us") is an independent iOS developer. Critter Camp (the "App") is designed for kids ages 6–12 and intended for use under parental supervision.

2. Information the App does not collect

  • No name, address, email, phone number, or government identifier.
  • No precise location, contacts, photos, calendar, microphone, camera, or health data.
  • No social-login data (we do not offer Sign in with Apple or third-party logins).
  • No advertising identifiers (IDFA / IDFV). We do not request App Tracking Transparency permission because we do not track.
  • No third-party advertising or behavioral profiling SDKs.

3. The minimal data we do use

To operate the App, the following data is processed:

  • Anonymous usage events via PostHog. We configure PostHog to disable advertising identifiers, anonymize IP addresses, and not capture device identifiers. Events tell us things like "a session was completed" — never who completed it.
  • Crash diagnostics via PostHog (same configuration as above). Helps us fix bugs.
  • Subscription status via RevenueCat and the Apple App Store. RevenueCat receives an anonymous user ID (a random UUID assigned by the App, not by you) and your purchase status. Apple handles all payment information; we never see card or payment details.
  • On-device-only state (your child's progress, Cammy's berries, daily streak) stays on the device using Apple's SwiftData. It is never uploaded.

4. COPPA / children's privacy

Although Critter Camp is offered in the standard App Store (not the Kids category), the App is designed to align with the U.S. Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and similar laws. Specifically:

  • We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from anyone, including children.
  • The App contains no in-app advertising, no behavioral profiling, and no social networking features.
  • All commerce (subscription purchase, restore, external links from Settings) is gated behind a parental challenge that a typical 6–12-year-old cannot solve unaided.
  • If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided personal information through Critter Camp, please contact us at support@n5labs.com and we will promptly delete it.

5. Subscriptions

Critter Camp is free to download. An optional subscription (Critter Camp Pro) is available at $4.99/month or $29.99/year (U.S. pricing; localized in other regions). A 7-day free trial may be offered. Purchases are processed by Apple; renewals, refunds, and cancellations are managed via your Apple ID account settings. We do not have access to your payment details.

6. Third-party services

  • Apple App Store — purchase, refund, and renewal of subscriptions.
  • RevenueCat — subscription validation and entitlement management. RevenueCat privacy policy.
  • PostHog — anonymous product analytics. We self-configure to disable identifiers and anonymize IP. PostHog privacy policy.

7. Data retention and your rights

Anonymous analytics events are retained by PostHog for up to 12 months. RevenueCat retains subscription records as long as your subscription is active and for a reasonable period afterward for tax / compliance purposes. To request review, correction, or deletion of any record associated with your subscription, email support@n5labs.com and we will respond within 30 days.

Depending on your jurisdiction (EU/EEA under GDPR, California under CCPA/CPRA, Israel, UK), you have the right to access, correct, delete, port, restrict, or object to processing of your data, and to lodge a complaint with your data protection authority. Use the email above to exercise these rights.

8. Data security

Data in transit is protected by industry-standard TLS. No method of transmission is 100% secure; we cannot guarantee absolute security, but we apply commercially reasonable safeguards.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will revise the effective date at the top and, where required, notify you in the App. Continued use of the App after a change constitutes acceptance.
