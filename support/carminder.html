Need help with CarMinder widgets, lyrics cards, motion posters, or ride sounds? Email support@n5labs.com.

Common setup notes

  • Widgets are managed by iOS and WidgetKit. Add CarMinder widgets from the iPhone widget picker where iOS supports widgets.
  • Car image widgets use the image you choose in CarMinder and store a widget-safe copy on your device.
  • Lyrics cards use text you enter or own. CarMinder does not include a licensed lyrics library.
  • Ride sounds use Apple Shortcuts automations and local notification sounds. Sound playback can depend on notification permission, Silent Mode, Focus modes, and volume.
